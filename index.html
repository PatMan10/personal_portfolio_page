ABOUT

I'm a recent university graduate who is fascinated by computing technology because of how it has augmented communication and information interchange among humans; and how quickly computers evolved over the past 25 years; and how rapidly the evolution continues to grow.

During university, I learned the fundamental software development concepts using mainly Microsoft technologies like Windows, C#, asp.NET and Java, but I discovered Linux a year ago and I started to explore it. After several months of exploration, I found that I very much prefer Linux based operating systems for work to Windows because of the more open philosophy and the benefits thereof.

During my exploration of Linux, I managed to learn several new technologies that I really enjoy working with, like how to use the terminal, how to write Bash scripts, Nodejs, Reactjs and native Android development.

Given that I am an analytical, technical and pragmatic person by nature, software development was a natural path for me, which is why I do it and like it so much. When I'm not in cyberspace or coding I enjoy reading, socialising with friends and/or family, and playing football or rugby for fun.

I enjoy designing and building great apps.

I can help in all stages of the SDLC ranging from initial concept development, design, construction, testing and deployment.
